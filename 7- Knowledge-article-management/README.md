##  Scenario
The project team needs to publish an article that explains how to enroll in the pilot program in the IT Knowledge Base.
Appropriate members of the IT department will be granted authoring permissions. Article should remains secure and accessible only to employees  

As the system administrator, I will create a group and assign specific roles to users to manage access to knowledge management. 

## Objective
Create a group and assign specific roles to users to manage access to knowledge management

## Tasks Completed
- Create knowledge management group
- Add users to group
- Add roles to knowledge group
- Update knowledgebase workflow
- Update user criteria to knowledge base
- Create knowledge article
- Approve knowledge article


## Result
A knowledge base group created speifically for knowledge articles only accessible by employee.
